Simple NTP

BRING A FRESH NEW LOOK TO YOUR CHROME'S NEW TAB PAGE.

Simple NTP is a Chrome extension which brings an all new exprience to your Chrome New Tab Page with rich information that the inbuilt one should have provided. Weather for your location, Bookmarks, Recently visits, News and recent Downloads, all are displayed in Material Design cards and ready when you open any new tabs. You don't have to go through many menu items to find a bookmark, or press key combination to open your history page. They are right there when Chrome launches or a new tab is created.

It now works with any Chromium-based browsers including Chrome and the new Chromium Edge.

Want more control? Visit Settings to change default background and temperature unit to Farenheit.

NOTE: Simple NTP needs your location and uses cookies to request and save weather info. That's all. It does not collect any personal data of yours.
