LINKLZ

What exactly does Linklz do? It simply helps you remove URL redirection, for example https://somewhere.net/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.example.com   ➡  https://www.example.com, and also eliminate annnoying tracking parameters in URL so the target website in the URL finds it harder to track your activity, like: https://www.example.com/?utm_source=somewhere&hl=en  ➡   https://www.example.com/&hl=en.

You can also shorten the URL after getting rid of those params. Though that elimination is not perfect, it just makes the tracking process become more difficult. The recommended method is shorten URL after cleaning. Then you can share the newly processed link on social networks like Twitter and Instagram, or generate QR Code based on it.

QR generator is based on the New URL. If New URL field is empty, it will use Input URL instead.
