LINKLZ

What exactly does Linklz do? It simply helps you eliminate annnoying tracking parameters in URL so the target website in the URL finds it harder to track your activity, for example: http://www.example.com/?utm_source=somewhere&hl=en   ➡   http://www.example.com/&hl=en

You can also shorten the URL after getting rid of those params. Though that elimination is not perfect, it just makes the tracking process become more difficult. The recommended method is shorten URL after removing the tracking params.

Remove tracking params only removes things such as "utm_source", "clickfrom" and "ref" and keeps other non-tracking params like "q=your+query", "id=123abc" and "hl=en" like what you can see in the example above.

URL shortener is powered by https://is.gd. Special thanks to Mr. Richard West for his amazing work, and Memset who their awesome support that makes is.gd free of charge.
